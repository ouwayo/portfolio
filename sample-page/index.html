About Me

To whom it may concern

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am writing to introduce myself and express special interest for in the field of technology. I believe that my experience and education make me a competitive candidate worthy of consideration.

I am currently a Junior at Ashesi University, majoring in Computer Engineering.
However, besides academia, I am a member of Code the Change club, we solve
problems in the community using technology. Through this group, I am working on
a hostel management system that will help the on-campus students to be able to
register online; and raise their concerns without having the struggle to meet
the school officials.

I previously held an internship at the Rwanda Information Society Authority, which focused on Computer Networking and Web Development. This experience also includes working with the team to brainstorm new ideas and solve complex problems in the organization’s IT infrastructure. I believe that my self-motivation, adaptability, and a strong commitment to
service could be of interest to you. I would appreciate the opportunity to contribute to your ongoing growth and continued success as I also build a career of my dream. In addition, in my freshman year, I worked on the weather prediction model which aimed to help farmers in the Western Region of Ghana to know when to grow crops that are sensitive to weather. Specifically, I designed a Web application to spread information to extension officers who oversee farmers. During this project, I learned data analysis and web development in-depth, especially back end development.

Moreover, I possess excellent problem-solving skills and my Software Engineering enthusiasm makes me an ideal candidate for this position. I will work to meet the needs while representing my position with the utmost professionalism.

I look forward to hearing from you to further discuss the position and my qualifications.

Yours faithfully,

 Oscar Uwayo
